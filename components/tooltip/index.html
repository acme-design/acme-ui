API

参数(*为必填)说明类型默认值
children*子元素ReactNode
content*提示内容ReactNode
width提示框的最大宽度string | number
trigger提示框的触发方式, 目前只支持hover/click/focus"hover" | "click" | "focus" | ("hover" | "click" | "focus")[] hover
openDelay提示框出现的延时时间，单位：毫秒number 100
placement提示框位置 "top" | "left" | "right" | "bottom" | "auto" | "auto-start" | "auto-end" | "top-start" | "top-end" | "bottom-start" | "bottom-end" | "right-start" | "right-end" | "left-start" | "left-end"auto
overlayClassName提示框类名string
overlayStyle提示框样式CSSProperties
onVisibleChange关闭弹层的回调函数((visible: boolean) => void)
visible控制popper是否展开boolean
boundary提示框翻转边界 默认为documentReactElement<any, string | ((props: any) => ReactElement<any, any> | null) | (new (props: any) => Component<any, any, any>)>
interactive鼠标移入popper是否保持提示框弹出boolean
